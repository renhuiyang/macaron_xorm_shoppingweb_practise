About Me
Congratulations. I’m Tania and prior to becoming a registered midwife I was a Teacher of the Deaf here in Christchurch. I have also been teaching antenatal classes at both Lincoln and Christchurch Women’s Hospitals for the past 11 years. I believe that when you are fully informed you are able to make the choices that best suit your journey which in turn will enable you to take the lead in your own care, birth and parenting. I will support you to birth at home, Lincoln Hospital, St Georges Hospital or at Christchurch Women’s Hospital. Antenatal appointments are held at the midwifery rooms at Halswell Village Midwives by appointment. I provide home visit postnatal care for both you and your baby for up to 6 weeks after your birth. I work closely with my practice partner Jessica Skinner. Please feel free to call me and have a chat about whether I might be the right midwife for you. I look forward to hearing from you.
