FAQ

What is laborelief?
Laborelief is a mouthpiece that you place in your mouth like a mouth guard. Each laborelief pack comes with four sizes so that you can find the size that provides you with the most pain relief during each stage of birth.
What are my options for pain management during labor? Can I use laborelief with other devices or birth methods?
Pain management during labor alternatives include breathing techniques, massage, epidurals, gas, TENS machines, and oxygen. Laborelief can be used alone or in conjunction with these birthing aides. In short whatever works – do!
Why use laborelief?
Laborelief is a natural way to reduce both pain in labor and time in labor. It is easy to use right out of the box and requires no wait time (methods such as epidural may require a long hospital wait). Laborelief is made from a thermoplastic elastomer and has no taste or smell.
Is laborelief natural?
Yes!
Laborelief is a completely natural aid to the safe birth of your baby. It is made from safe and tested materials and allows you to increase your core strength in the pushing phase of birth.
What science is there to back up laborelief?
DSD's or "Dental Support Devices" have been studied in regard to their effect on core strength and muscular power – two essential elements of your birth. In 2009 a study was conducted by the dept of Obstetrics and Gynecology at the University of Maryland. This study proved that labor time was reduced by 40% in the second stage of labor. The study also showed that the number of failure to descend incidents was reduced. The study further showed that by increasing oxygen intake, which laborelief assists in, there is a breakdown of lactic acid build up which increases core strength.
Can I speak with the laborelief device in my mouth?
Absolutely – laborelief is light and easy to wear. It does not interfere with speech. If you find that it is causing you to have too much saliva simply take it out between contractions.
Why do I get four laborelief mouthpieces?
Each laborelief order contains one box with 4 sizes (each one a different color and thickness). We supply laborelief in various sizes so that you can find the one that is most comfortable to wear in each stage of labor. Generally we recommend that you start with the thinnest and work your way up – but there is no need to change if you are happy with the one you are using and it is comfortable and providing you pain relief.
How do I wear the mouthpiece?
The laborelief can be worn on either your top teeth or bottom teeth, whichever is more comfortable.
